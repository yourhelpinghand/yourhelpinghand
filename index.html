Our Vision


Just discussing issues on social media and not focusing on the main problem. Nah that's not our style!


As we cut the drag and land on a solution, there remain many heroes working for the upliftment of society behind the name of NGOs and Social Activists. We focus on supporting these changemakers in order to build a community that can provide help to every corner of our nation.

Who we are?


A team of two college students aspiring to bring changes to the fullest of their potential.


Being familiar with technology and social media, which might not be a strong point of many NGOs and social activists, we decided to help them to spread their initiative and become helping hand of the helpers of society,

What we do?


Promoting the cause of the people for which they chose to step up and work for the society.


We make podcasts with the societal heroes in order to put their work in front of the world to transform more and more people from being a thinker to be a changemaker!

So tune in with us as when you tune in you take a step towards making a change!
